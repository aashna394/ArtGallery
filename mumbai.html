Welcome to Mumbai.

Currently ongoing, Mumbai's street art festival, also known as the "St+art Mumbai" festival, is an annual event that brings together talented street artists from India and around the world to showcase their creativity and transform public spaces into vibrant art installations. Through this festival, Mumbai's streets are transformed into an open-air gallery, showcasing the city's diverse art and cultural scene.
