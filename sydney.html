Welcome to Sydney

The Aboriginal art exhibition curated by students in Sydney showcases the rich cultural heritage of the Indigenous Australians through a diverse range of artworks. From traditional paintings and sculptures to contemporary installations, the exhibition provides a platform for Indigenous artists to share their stories and perspectives with a wider audience. Through this exhibition, students aim to promote cross-cultural understanding and appreciation of Aboriginal art and culture.
